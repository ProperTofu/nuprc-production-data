Definitions
Oil & Volume Units
bbls Barrels — standard unit of crude oil volume (42 US gallons / ~159 litres)
M bbls Million barrels — used to measure annual or terminal-level production
bopd Barrels of Oil Per Day — daily production rate derived from monthly volumes
Cond. Condensate — ultra-light hydrocarbon liquid produced alongside natural gas
Tn 1,000,000 MMscf of gas — used in this dashboard for cumulative gas totals
Gas Units & Terms
MMscf Million Standard Cubic Feet — primary unit of natural gas measurement
Bscf Billion Standard Cubic Feet — 1,000 MMscf; used for larger reserves
AG Associated Gas — natural gas produced alongside crude oil from the same reservoir
NAG Non-Associated Gas — gas from dedicated gas reservoirs, not co-produced with oil
Field Field Use — gas consumed on-site for power, drilling, and operational needs
Regulatory & ESG
NUPRC Nigerian Upstream Petroleum Regulatory Commission — successor to DPR, regulates upstream operations
Flare Flaring — combustion of associated gas at the wellhead; measured against ZEFLOC 2030 target
IOC International Oil Company — multinationals such as Shell, TotalEnergies, and ExxonMobil
NOC National Oil Company — NNPC Ltd and its subsidiary operating companies
ZEFLOC Zero Flaring by 2030 — Nigeria's national commitment to eliminate routine gas flaring
Dashboard Terms
YoY Year-on-Year — percentage change comparing a year to the immediately prior year
Decline Decline Rate — % reduction in production volume relative to a prior reference period
Util % Utilization % — share of total gas produced that was sold or used operationally
Term. Terminal — export facility where oil is metered, stored, and loaded onto tankers
Comm% Commercialization Rate — proportion of gas produced that is sold or used productively vs flared
