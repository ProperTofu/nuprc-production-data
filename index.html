National Oil & Gas Production Intelligence Dashboard
2020–2026 · Oil & Gas Production

Nigeria's upstream petroleum sector produced a cumulative 3.61 billion barrels of oil and condensate between January 2020 and April 2026, with 13.91 trillion MMscf of gas recorded from 2021 onwards. Output peaked in 2020 at 671 million barrels and declined 17.7% by 2025. Gas commercialization improved from 90.3% to 92.7% while the flare rate fell from 9.2% to 6.7%. All data sourced from official NUPRC Monthly Production Reports.
